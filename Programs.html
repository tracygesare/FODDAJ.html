Learn a Skill to earn

Our Mentorship program helps mentees to acquire new market relevant skills, resources, links and information to boost careers and businesses.

There are hundreds of girls and women that have received hand on support in their career path and business growth and built productive mentoring relationships with a community of impact mentors. Programs offered include:

  • Tailoring and Dressmaking
  • Computer Literacy
  • Beauty and hair dressing

    FORUM FOR WOMEN IN DEVELOPMENT, DEMOCRACY AND JUSTICE in Ngong, Kenya conducts training of local women and youth groups, institutions, and leadership on women’s rights, GBV response and prevention, advocacy to equip women with knowledge to strengthen local structures in gender equality.

    FODDAJ applies coherent strategies to activism through lobbying and advocacy as well as research and documentation of GBV trends in traditional communities.

    Forum for Women works to demolish the very foundation of prejudice against women and their rights. Our philosophy is simple. We attack the underpinnings of violence against women by debunking and discrediting myths and countering of cultural systems that discriminate against women. By chipping away at the underlying ignorance that fuels negative attitudes, we can ultimately win our fight for fairness and achieve full equality for women and men in Kenya.”

    We provide victims of sexual abuse, domestic violence victims, and Victims of Human Trafficking with crisis counseling and material-based support, case management, and referrals for emergency health services. We conduct counseling and placement with support groups to help the recovery and resilience of survivors of violence.
