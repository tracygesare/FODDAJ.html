Mission Overview

Written by: Tracy Mokweri on 10/10/2024 9:00am

"FODDAJ (Forum for Women in Development Democracy and Justice) is a community based Organizaton established with the aim of promoting women's rights, supporting democaracy movements and promoting social justice in Kenya. We envison a society in which women activley engage in both development and governance."
